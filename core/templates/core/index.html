Nurturing the
Young Minds

The Vagbhatananda Edu Project, initiated by the ULCCS Foundation under the Uralungal Labour Contract Co-operative Society ,has been enhancing education since 2010. This initiative operates through eight regional centers in Kozhikode district and is supported by various volunteer groups. The primary goal of the project is to provide school students with innovative curricular and co-curricular experiences and prepare them for competitive exams.
