About us

Malabar Antique were established in 1989 at Thazheyangadi the Old Vadakara town. We take great pride in continuing the tradition established by our parents, Malabar Antique is currently operated by Mr. Majeed.


After that, in 2016, another initiative called Craft Decor started funtioning in Iringal near by National Highway 66 (Vadakara - Kozhikode route). It works well with a large number of items in a spacious building.


We continue to import all of our merchandise from All over India. We receive shipments several times a year, attracting customers from all regions of the India and abroad who come to Malabar Antique to furnish their interiors.


We remain the business whose expertise in collecting and selling quality antiques is world famous. We are indeed fortunate to have such an outstanding collection of authentic Indian and Colonial pieces.
